Project Description

Simply Playgrounds designs and installs bespoke outdoor play areas for schools and councils. The platform showcases playground equipment, safety surfacing and fitness installations, emphasising quality materials, safety compliance and custom designs.

Simply Playgrounds is a UK‑based playground design and installation company specializing in creating safe, engaging outdoor play environments for schools, councils, nurseries, and private clients. The platform showcases a wide range of bespoke wooden playground equipment, play panels, fitness stations, playground markings, and safety surfacing, all tailored to suit different age groups and budgets. The service emphasizes custom designs, high‑quality materials, and adherence to safety standards to ensure durable, fun, and educational play spaces. Simply Playgrounds also offers installation and ongoing support, helping clients bring their playground visions to life—from initial planning to final setup—across the UK.
