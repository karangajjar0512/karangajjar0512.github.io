Project Description

Flames Fireplace Centre has been a leading provider of fireplaces, fires, and stoves in Wakefield and surrounding areas (Barnsley, Dewsbury, Huddersfield, and Leeds) for nearly two decades. Our showroom offers over 50 live displays, showcasing a wide variety of traditional and contemporary fireplace designs, including stone, marble, and wood mantels, as well as gas, electric, and solid fuel options. We pride ourselves on offering both ready-made and bespoke fireplace solutions to suit any style or home.

We work with quality British suppliers, many of whom are local to Yorkshire, and consistently update our product range to stay ahead of trends. With free parking and a welcoming, knowledgeable team, we aim to make the fireplace selection and installation process as smooth as possible. Whether you’re looking for a classic design or a modern, remote-controlled fire, our team helps turn your vision into reality.

Our service goes beyond products—we also provide expert installation with qualified, friendly professionals who ensure your fireplace is safely and perfectly fitted. Customers choose us for our commitment to quality, customer service, and long-lasting warmth for their homes.
