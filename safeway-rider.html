Project Description

Safeway Rider is a long-established driving school offering learner tuition and fleet driver training. It focuses on personalised coaching, theory support and safe driving practices to help students pass tests and build lifelong driving confidence.

Safeway Rider is a well‑established driving school and motoring instruction service based in Bradford, West Yorkshire, serving learners throughout West Yorkshire and beyond. Founded in 1992, it offers a wide range of driving tuition programs with experienced, patient instructors to help students safely pass their theory and practical driving tests and become confident drivers for life. The school also provides fleet driver training for companies to enhance safety, reduce accidents, and improve fuel efficiency, as well as specialized training materials and theory support. With a focus on high‑quality instruction, personalized coaching, and results‑oriented training, Safeway Rider helps thousands of learners achieve their driving goals in a supportive learning environment.
